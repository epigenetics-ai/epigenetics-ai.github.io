Epigenetic One

Unlock your body’s true age and future health risks with our app. Using just a blood test snapshot, we deliver rapid, accurate biological age and disease risk scores. Empowering health-conscious users with personalized insights and region-specific biomarkers for proactive lifestyle changes and slowed aging. Available on iOS and Android.

Epigenetic Together

Our AI platform optimizes prognosis and care pathways by analyzing epigenetic and genetic data. We identify care gaps and recommend personalized lifestyle interventions at scale for healthcare providers and insurers. Our unique algorithms integrate diverse population data, especially Asian cohorts, enhancing regional clinical effectiveness. Delivered as a cloud-based SaaS solution.

Epigenetic Bank

Our cloud platform accelerates research by curating and analyzing extensive Asian and Chinese epigenetic and genetic data. This unique resource is vital for advancing precision medicine and drug development for these populations. Access via subscription and collaborations. A first in Asia, filling a critical global research gap.
